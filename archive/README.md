## A note on archival policy

A product lands here when:

- It's no longer maintained or featured in the dApp's primary surfaces.
- The team has explicitly decided not to continue developing it.
- Its functionality is fully superseded by another product, or the use case no longer makes business sense.

Archived products may still have on-chain state (active LPs, locked funds, open positions). Each page includes the **withdrawal / recovery instructions** for users who still have something there.

We don't take down archive pages. They stay as a public record of what shipped and why it didn't continue.
